Overview

Great End Unit With Wrap-around Balcony

Our gorgeous 3 bedroom Sand Castle II beachfront condo on Florida's Suncoast is available for those wanting one of the best accommodations in the Tampa / St. Petersburg area. Located directly on Indian Shores just 30 minutes from the Tampa International Airport.

Sandy beaches, extending for miles, can be viewed from your private balcony. Imagine sipping you favorite drink in the evening while watching one of the most beautiful seaside sunsets anywhere. Wake up in the morning with the gentle sound of the surf drifting through the master suite patio doors.

Enjoy a few laps in the heated swimming pool or unwind in a poolside Jacuzzi. Take a walk, jog, or bike the miles of tranquil beach. Hungry? Don't feel like going out for dinner, create your own at one of our gas barbecues in the delightful poolside picnic area adjacent to one of the largest sundecks on the beach.

We are located in Indian Shores, one of the quieter beach areas with less traffic, but still very close to many restaurants and shops. The famous Crabby Bill's and Salt Rock Grill restaurants are nearby and well worth a visit. The excitement of Disney World and Universal Studios is just 90 minutes away, while Busch Gardens is nearby in Tampa.
